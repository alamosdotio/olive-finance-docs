# Receiving Pool Token

Steps:

1.  Go to earn page
2. Click on the liquidity pool. there is an interface with an option to buy and sell. click buy.
3. Select the token you'd like to trade for the liquidity pool
4. Decide the amount that youd like to trade for the liqudity pool
5. A module will appear at the bootmom showing the estimated quantity of liquidity pool and the fees for buying into the pool
6. A notification asking for confirmation of the transaction will appear. confirm the transactrion
7. You will recieve a message on the succes of transaction
